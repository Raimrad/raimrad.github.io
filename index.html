Briefly about Kazakhstan:

Kazakhstan is a unique multinational country where more than 130 ethnic groups coexist in peace and harmony. Its population consists of representatives of various cultures, languages and religions, which makes it one of the most multinational states in the world. And also in Kazakhstan there are amazing creations of nature - relict forests, deep lakes, numerous rivers, high mountains, endless steppes, as well as rare species of animals and plants.
